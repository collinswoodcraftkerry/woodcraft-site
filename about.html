About Us

Collins Woodcraft started trading in 2003. Micheal has since been building a team of experienced, dedicated carpenters and developing a broad base of happy customers across Kerry/Munster in both mobile and computer.

The continued expansion in capacity and specialist expertise gives our clients a broader range of services. Micheal's personal commitment to high standards, innovation and implementation of cutting edge techniques ensures unrivalled customer service throughout the process and a top quality final product.

Collins Woodcraft has a growing reputation for delivering projects within budget with efficiency and attention to detail as a focus.

We are committed to offering exceptional quality of work in all areas of construction both large and small. If you have a project you would like us to work on, we would love to hear from you.
